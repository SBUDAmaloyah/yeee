True Hermeneutics

What is in this book?

The word Hermeneutics derives from the Greek “hermeneuo” is used to reflect, (a) the study and statement of the principles on which a text (for our purposes is the biblical text) is to be understood, (b) or the interpretation of the text in such a way that its message comes home to the reader

I offer this work to simplify the scriptures in the Bible that seem to be difficult for most readers to comprehend. For example, Jesus offered many parables that do not always make sense to the lay man or new Christians, or to those who did not grow up in a Christian environment. Please note that all the scriptures and verses quoted here are taken from the King James version of the Bible. I have been motivated and encouraged by the Holy Spirit to prepare and share with you the unbelievable riches of the gospel of Jesus Christ. I do this in a simple manner.

I want you to put on a safety belt and prepare yourself to be “mind-blown” by the information contained in this book. This is not just ordinary information you are about to read, it is an eye opener, offering the greatest spiritual awareness of all time. To give you a brief summary: there are mysteries, such as what makes us human sick? Who Satan is and how he operates in his dark kingdom.

You will also find the true definition of who God, the creator and how he relates to his son Jesus. There is also a chapter that speaks about spiritual gifts, which will help you as an individual grow in the knowledge of God. To those who have been wondering about the significance of tithe in the church, they will get the answers.
